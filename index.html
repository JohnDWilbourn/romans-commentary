Romans Road

The Epistle of Paul to the Romans

This commentary works through Paul's letter to the Romans verse by verse, with continuous attention to the Greek text of the NA28 critical edition. Each chapter develops the exegesis in full — grammatical analysis, doctrinal content, historical background, and application — from Romans 1:1 through the conclusion of the epistle. It is written for the serious reader who wants to know what the text actually says and what it means.
